## 🎯 How to Upload Your Mixes

### Step 1: Access the Upload Button
- Look for the purple **"Upload Music"** button in the bottom-right corner of your website
- Click it to open the upload form

### Step 2: Fill Out the Upload Form
1. **Audio File** (Required)
   - Click to select your MP3, WAV, or other audio file
   - Maximum file size: 500MB per file
   - Supported formats: MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, etc.

2. **Track Title** (Required)
   - Enter the name of your mix
   - Example: "PRIMERCE FRESH HITS #2026 DJ ENOCH VOL4 DANCEHALL"

3. **Mix Type** (Required)
   - Choose between:
     - **Latest Mix** - For recent tracks and new releases
     - **Non-Stop Mix** - For longer continuous mixes

4. **Duration** (Optional)
   - Enter the track length
   - Example: "45:30" or "1:30:00"

5. **Release Date** (Optional)
   - Select when the mix was released
   - Defaults to today's date

### Step 3: Upload
- Click the **"Upload Track"** button
- Wait for the upload to complete (progress will show)
- Once done, your track will appear on the Downloads section automatically!
