## 💰 Payment Flow

```
User selects product
    ↓
Redirected to /payment
    ↓
Clicks "Pay with PesaPal"
    ↓
Redirected to PesaPal portal
    ↓
Chooses payment method (Airtel/MTN/Card)
    ↓
Completes payment
    ↓
Returns to app
    ↓
Payment auto-verified
    ↓
✅ Payment approved
    ↓
Content access granted
```
