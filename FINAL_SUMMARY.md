## 🎯 Payment Workflow

### User Journey
```
User clicks "Buy Now"
    ↓ (30 seconds)
Payment page loads with summary
    ↓
User clicks "Pay with PesaPal"
    ↓
Redirected to PesaPal portal
    ↓ (2-3 minutes)

    ↓

    ↓ (1 second)
Backend verifies payment
```
