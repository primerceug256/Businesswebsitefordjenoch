# Quick Start Guide - Payment System

## 🎯 For Users

### How to Purchase a Subscription:
1. Go to Movies or Subscription page
2. Click "Buy Now" on desired plan
3. You're taken to **Payment page**
4. Review your order
5. Click **"Pay with PesaPal"**
6. Choose payment method:
   - Airtel Money (preferred in Uganda)
   - MTN Mobile Money
   - Credit/Debit Card
7. Complete payment
8. **Automatically redirected** to your library
9. Start watching!

### How to Check Payment History:
1. Click **Menu** (hamburger icon)
2. Go to **Account** section
3. Click **"Payments"**
4. See all your purchases
5. Options:
   - **Download Receipt** - Get PDF
   - **Request Refund** - Ask for money back
   - **View Details** - See what you bought

### How to Request a Refund:
1. Go to **Menu** → **Payments**
2. Find payment you want refunded
3. Click **"View Details"**
4. Click **"Request Refund"**
5. Confirm
6. Admin will review and process
7. **You'll get refund notification email**

---

## 🔧 For Admin

### How to View All Payments:
1. Login as admin
2. Click **Menu** (hamburger)
3. Scroll to **Admin** section
4. Click **"Payments Dashboard"**
5. See **stats cards**:
   - Total Revenue
   - Total Payments
   - Approved Count
   - Pending Count

### How to Manage Pending Payments:
1. Go to **Payments Dashboard**
2. Click **Filter** → Select **"Pending"**
3. Review each payment:
   - Click row to expand
   - See customer info
   - See items purchased
4. Click **"Approve"** or **"Reject"**
5. Customer gets email notification

### How to Process a Refund:
1. Go to **Payments Dashboard**
2. Search for customer email or name
3. Click payment row to expand
4. Click **"Refund"** button
5. Confirm refund
6. **Customer receives refund email**
7. Payment marked as "refunded"

### How to Export Payment Data:
1. Go to **Payments Dashboard**
2. Click **"Export to CSV"** button
3. CSV file downloads
4. Open in Excel or Google Sheets
5. Contains:
   - Customer name & email
   - Amount paid
   - Payment method
   - Payment date
   - Status

### How to Search Payments:
1. Use **Search bar** at top
2. Search by:
   - Customer name
   - Customer email
   - Payment ID
3. Results update in real-time

### How to Filter Payments:
1. Click **Filter dropdown**
2. Select:
   - **All** - Show everything
   - **Approved** - Completed payments
   - **Pending** - Waiting for review
   - **Rejected** - Declined payments
   - **Refunded** - Money returned

## 📊 Payment Status Meanings

| Status | Meaning | Action Needed |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| **Approved** ✅ | Payment successful | None - customer has access |
| **Pending** ⏳ | Awaiting review | Click "Approve" or "Reject" |
| **Rejected** ❌ | Payment declined | Contact customer to retry |
| **Refunded** 💰 | Money returned | Archive/close ticket |

## ⚠️ Common Issues

### "I don't see my payment in history"
- Payment may still be processing
- Try refreshing page
- Check filter isn't hiding it
- Wait a few minutes

### "Refund button is grayed out"
- Only approved payments can be refunded
- Pending/rejected payments can't be refunded
- Contact user first if unsure

### "Email notification didn't arrive"
- Check spam folder
- Email system is configured
- Verify admin email in settings
- May be queued for sending

### "Payment stuck on PesaPal page"
- Complete the transaction
- Or go back and try again
- Contact support if amount was charged
